I tell everyone that JSU is a fine institution.

by Rachel B. Morgan - 1987

I was a graduate of JSUs RN to BSN program in 1987. I thoroughly enjoyed my experience at JSU. Just before graduating I was offered a temporary position as a clinical instructor for the community health program. This gave me my first experience as a teacher.

I was preparing to Move to Hawaii that spring with my husband who was in the service then. One of my former professors knew we were in a dilemma about what to do with our 2 cocker spaniels and she asked if she and her son could adopt them so they wouldn't have to live in quarantine for 6 months alone. I felt it was an answer to prayer since they went to a loving home. I've been working in Washington DC as a lobbyist for 17 years now representing state legislators and state governments on health and human services policy, which is extremely active at present. I tell everyone that JSU is a fine institution.
